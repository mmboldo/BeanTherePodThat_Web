Bean There Pod That

Coffee Time

Bean There Pod That is an application developed to help coffee lovers to keep track of the coffees they have tasted. It is a community-powered platform for personalized recommendations to all coffee fanatics. The Bean There Pod That will present the user with a database of all types of coffees available in the market. The users will be able to rate the coffee they tasted on a scale of one to five pods. The community dimension is what makes it a truly customer-centric platform. It allows users to rate the coffees and read other users' comments.

Offering customized recommendations depending on their past behavior, the more ratings and reviews they leave on the app, the better their recommendations. Bean There Pod That seems to be the perfect conjunction big data, and machine learning assisting coffee lovers never to be disappointed ever again with their choices and open their horizons to different experiences and tastes.
